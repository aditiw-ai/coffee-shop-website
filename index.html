Our Philosophy

Coffee is our
Craft & Calling

Grounded was born from a simple belief — that truly great coffee deserves unhurried attention. We work directly with small-batch farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, paying above fair-trade prices to ensure sustainable livelihoods.

In our roastery, every batch is hand-crafted in small quantities to highlight the unique terroir of each origin. From bean selection to the final pour, we obsess over every detail.
